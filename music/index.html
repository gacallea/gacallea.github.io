Music

I am an electronic music musician and producer with my own record label: Gentle Wash Records. I am self-taught and I compose electronic music by intuition. My music has been praised by magazines, at festivals and in theatres. Proudly aired on WFMU, featured on The Wire, Resonance FM, and Future Ecologies.

I publish under two monikers with different styles:

  • Aner Andros: a left-field, darker, and introspective aesthetic.

  • GKRGY: focusing on the rhythmic and brighter side of tunes.

Over the years I have performed sporadically and with great enjoyment. I’m fond of improvisation and of bespoke live shows. Using empathy and intuition as critical factors in the making of musical voyages.
